Chapter 2 Introduction

This is the analysis report for the conjoint experiment of the Good Loser Project by Peter Esaiasson, Hannah Werner, and Sveinung Arnesen. The experiment was fielded in Norway during the fall of 2018 through the 13th wave of Norwegian Citizen Panel (NCP). The NCP is a research-purpose internet panel with over 6000 active participants. It is based on a probability sample of the general Norwegian population above the age of 18 drawn from the Norwegian National Registry. The survey is based on a online questionnaire with postal recruitment. Panel members complete a questionnaire three times a year of 15 minutes each. The NCP is a core component of The Digital Social Science Core Facilities (DIGSSCORE), and was established in 2013 as a collaboration between several departments at the Faculty of Social Sciences at the University of Bergen and NORCE â€“ Norwegian Research Centre.
